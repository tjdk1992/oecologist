iPhone 6 @ Lake Biwa, Shiga, Japan

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Field Science Education and Research Center in Kyoto University (Project Researcher) and at the Center for Southeast Asian Area Studies in Kyoto University (Affiliate Researcher)

My research

My research focuses on invasion success of alien fish species and their ecological and socioeconomic impacts in highly anthropogenically altered landscapes in Southeast Asia, particularly in the monsoon deltas of the Indochina peninsula. Currently, I am mostly focusing on evaluating impacts of alien Cichlid fishes on the ichthyofauna and on the traditional fish resource use by local people in the lowermost Chao Phraya River basin, Thailand
